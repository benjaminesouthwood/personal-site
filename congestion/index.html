UK City Road Congestion

Multi-source dashboard comparing congestion data from DfT, INRIX, and TomTom

London Birmingham Manchester Leeds Sheffield Liverpool Glasgow Nottingham/Derby Newcastle/Sunderland
DfT CGN0504: Average delay on local 'A' roads (seconds per vehicle mile). England only - excludes Glasgow. For combined areas, values require flow-weighting by vehicle-miles.
INRIX Traffic Scorecard: Hours lost to congestion per driver per year. Based on INRIX's analysis of vehicle movement data across road networks.
TomTom Traffic Index: Congestion level as percentage of extra travel time. A 30% level means trips take 30% longer than in free-flow conditions.

Data sources: Department for Transport, INRIX Traffic Scorecard, TomTom Traffic Index
